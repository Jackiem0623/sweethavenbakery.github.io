Our Mission
At Sweet Haven Bakery, our mission is to create a place where people feel at home the moment they walk through the door. We believe great baking starts with simple, honest ingredients and the intention to brighten someone’s day. Every pastry, loaf, and latte is crafted by hand, using local products whenever possible and recipes passed down through generations. We’re here to serve our community—one warm muffin, fresh loaf, or friendly smile at a time.
Specials 
Start your week with something sweet! Every Monday, all lattes are half off when paired with any pastry. On Wednesdays, we host our Cupcake Decorating Hour, where guests can create their own custom treats with guidance from our bakers. And don’t forget our Saturday Morning Bread Walk, where fresh loaves come out of the oven every hour from 8 AM to noon. There’s always something happening at Sweet Haven Bakery to make your week a little brighter.
News 
Sweet Haven Bakery is excited to share that we’re rolling out a brand-new seasonal menu featuring maple pecan croissants, cinnamon swirl brioche, and our customer-favorite pumpkin cream cold brew. We’ve also recently expanded our kitchen, allowing us to bake larger batches while keeping the same handcrafted quality. And in even bigger news, we’re officially partnering with Harvest Home Farms to bring in fresh, locally sourced dairy and produce for our pastries. Stay tuned as more delicious updates are on the way!
