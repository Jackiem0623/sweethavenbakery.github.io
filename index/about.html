About Sweet Haven Bakery

Welcome to Heritage Bakery, where tradition meets taste in every delicious bite. For over 40 years, our family-run bakery has been a beloved staple in Livonia, serving up a tempting array of freshly made donuts, cakes, breads, paczkis, pastries, quiches, cookies, and more. We take pride in our commitment to quality, using time-honored recipes and the finest ingredients to create treats that are sure to satisfy your sweet cravings.

At Sweet Haven Bakery, we believe that every bite should be a delightful experience. That's why our bakers pour their passion and expertise into crafting each and every one of our delectable offerings. From our fluffy donuts to our decadent cakes, you can taste the love and care that goes into every bite. Whether you're stopping by for a morning coffee and pastry or picking up a special dessert for a celebration, our bakery has something for everyone.

As a family-owned business, we understand the importance of creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere for our customers. When you step through the doors of Sweet Haven Bakery, you're not just entering a bakery – you're joining our extended family. Our friendly staff is always ready to greet you with a smile and help you find the perfect treat to satisfy your cravings. We take pride in providing excellent customer service and creating a memorable experience for each and every visitor.
